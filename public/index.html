Genetic Algorithms

  • Not a singular algorithm, it's a class of algorithms
  • No understanding of biological sciences required

Taxonomy

There are 5 properties I've noticed that are required to classify an algorithm as a "Genetic Algorithm"

  • Genotypes / Phenotypes
  • Crossover (through mating)
  • Selection
  • Fitness
  • Mutation
